Money Management

This tool will help you to size your positions on your trades.

Position sizing is important to manage money effectively and minimize the losses.

How to interpret the results:

The result will be the amount of money you should put into the stock and the Stop Loss price.

Setting the stop loss price is a very good practice as it limits your loss no matter what.

If you exit the trade at the specified stop loss price, the effect of the stock price falling will be minimal for you.
