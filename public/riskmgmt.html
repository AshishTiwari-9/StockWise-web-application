Risk Management

Here you can identify the risks related to the stocks that you are interested in.

The risk is identified by the % values of expectancy of each trade.

How to interpret the results:

The results will be a percentage value for each scrip.

If the result is x% for a scrip, it means that for every trade being made at that price, you gain x% amount of your invested value.

If the result is negative, then executing that trade should be avoided, as you will loose x% amount of your invested amount for every trade.
